## 🛡️ Technical Architecture & Agent Integration

Since we execute locally under **Antigravity**, all authentication and posting credentials remain isolated on the user's secure system:

1. **LinkedIn Direct Share API**: Registers local OAuth credentials in `LINKEDIN_ACCESS_TOKEN` for POST operations to `https://api.linkedin.com/v2/ugcPosts`.
2. **Meta Graph Content API**: Uses long-lived Meta Graph system user tokens (`INSTAGRAM_ACCESS_TOKEN`, `THREADS_ACCESS_TOKEN`) to publish to Instagram Business and Threads accounts.
3. **Local Queue Worker (`queue.sqlite`)**: The backend queue is audited automatically by our process mapping logic, ensuring keyword compliance and platform-specific character limits before posting.
