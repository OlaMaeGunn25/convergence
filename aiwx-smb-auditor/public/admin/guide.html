📋 SMB Auditor Legend: Reading the Scores

This legend details the criteria behind each computed metric, how they drive pre-sales acquisition loops, and exactly what each security and filing classification represents.

1. What is actually being evaluated?

When you deploy an audit node, our pipeline executes a multi-layered check covering five critical operational profiles:

  • 🛡️ Active Firewall & Edge Security Posture: Analyzes whether a Web Application Firewall (WAF) such as Cloudflare or AWS WAF is shielding the server. It also probes standard HTTP headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options, CORS) to locate injection vulnerabilities.
  • ⚖️ State & Federal Regulatory Registry Standing: Indexes legal statuses directly from state registrar databases (CA, DE, NY Secretary of State registries) and cross-references federal government registries (SEC EDGAR CIKs, IRS EIN tax registries, and SAM.gov active CAGE procurement contracts).
  • 💰 Financial & Workforce Estimation: Extracts firmographic parameters including estimated annual revenues, YoY growth levels, and staffing sizes, mapping potential budget allocations.
  • 📈 Compliance Benchmarking & Industry Maturity: Audits the target site against regulatory checklists (GDPR cookie frameworks, CCPA policies, data encryption scopes) to compute a composite Maturity Grade (from A+ down to F).
  • 👥 Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Workforce Transition: Assesses team job profiles under the hood to map precise, modular prompt engineering training courses to transition manual staff members into advanced AI Managers and Validators.

2. How this data powers Pre-Sales Research

Rather than running manual audits, this dashboard automates pre-sales intelligence to streamline lead qualification and outbound outreach:

  • 🎯 Pinpoint Gaps: Instantly locate missing technical modules (e.g., missing conversational support, lack of social retargeting pixels, monolithic CMS page load bottlenecks) to present instant solutions.
  • 💼 Align Scope & Pricing: Use the financial and workforce estimates to scale proposed contract sizes (e.g., matching a high-ticket PO PDF parser to a $12M B2B manufacturer, or low-code retention emails to a $1.8M e-commerce storefront).
  • ✍️ Tailored Outbound Copy-Paste Scripts: Leverage vertical-correct email scripts containing calculated business ROI values, observed gap analyses, and exact pricing proposals directly under the Sales Command Center, allowing consultants to copy outreaches to clipboard in one click.
  • 📄 Physical Handouts: Export styled, client-facing PDF briefs or Word documents (.docx) to serve as professional leave-behind handouts.

3. Technical Score Glossary

Security Headers Matrix:

  • • Edge WAF (Web Application Firewall): Proves if an edge proxy layer is filtering botnets and malicious requests. "No Edge WAF" means their server is directly vulnerable to hacking and scraping.
  • • Strict-Transport-Security (HSTS): Commands browsers to strictly access the site over secure, encrypted HTTPS protocols.
  • • Content-Security-Policy (CSP): A whitelist detailing allowed script sources. Prevents malicious Cross-Site Scripting (XSS) and code injection.
  • • X-Frame-Options: Shields the site from clickjacking attacks by preventing bad actors from framing the webpage invisibly.
  • • Access-Control-Allow-Origin (CORS): Controls cross-origin session requests. "Secure" prevents cookies and logins from leaking.

State & Federal Filings Index:

  • • Entity Status: Verifies if the legal corporation is actively registered and in good corporate standing with state registers.
  • • Federal EIN/SAM: Maps their federal employer tax ID and checks active SAM.gov registrations, proving readiness to bid on U.S. Federal Government contracts.
  • • CAGE Code: Commercial and Government Entity code. A unique ID required to supply goods and services to military and federal agencies.
  • • SEC CIK ID: Central Index Key. Proves active securities filings and regulatory corporate disclosures.

Scorecard Ratings:

  • • Maturity Grade (Composite Posture Score): An overall grade detailing system readiness. A grade of D or F indicates manual administrative friction, obsolete security headers, and high operational inefficiencies, making them a prime candidate for an upskilled technological overhaul.
