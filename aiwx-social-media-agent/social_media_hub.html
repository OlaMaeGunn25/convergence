📖 Hub Operations & Setup Guide

        
        

          
          

            

              🚀 1. Campaign Planning
            

            
    
              
  • Select your active campaign (e.g. Consultancy Sprints) from the dropdown.
    • 
              
  • Provide a prompt inside the AI Brief Generator and click Generate to draft the posts queue automatically.
    • 
              
  • Configure your Operational Objective and set the UTM Campaign Label (e.g. AI_Consulting_Audit).
    • 
              
  • Check the Auto-Scheduler checkbox, set the frequency, and click Schedule & Start Campaign to activate background auto-posting.
    • 
            

          

          
          

            

              📅 2. Visual Calendar & Editor
            

            
    
              
  • The unified calendar shows all scheduled campaign posts across all platforms. Use the top filters to select by channel.
    • 
              
  • Click any day cell or platform badge to reveal the Preview/Editor Drawer on the right.
    • 
              
  • Review the post copy: UTM redirect parameters matching your campaign and platform are automatically configured and appended to the links on load!
    • 
              
  • Click Approve Campaign Post to queue it for the Auto-Scheduler, or click ⚡ Direct Publish to post it immediately.
    • 
            

          

          
          

            

              ⚡ 3. Social Comments Inbox
            

            
    
              
  • The system automatically scans social channels in the background for new inbound comments.
    • 
              
  • New comments trigger high-visibility Windows Toast notifications inside the dashboard.
    • 
              
  • Click a comment in the inbox list to load it into the responder. Click Regenerate Draft to let the AI agent formulate a context-aware reply, then click Submit Reply.
    •
